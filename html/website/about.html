About Us

We're on a mission to make people’s lives saucy! Soy sauce has been such an essential for us growing up, we want to share this amazing stable to every one in the world! We collaborate with many different soy sauce makers around the world, bringing top quality soy sauces to all our customers!

We Value

We firmly believe in transparency in our business. We want to be able to communciate all the ingredients that are used and the process of how soy sauces is made. We hope that through being transparent we are able to build a strong relationship with our customers. We value feedback greatly as well!

"We always had soy sauce in the house growing up, it was one of those childhood food staples. But as we grew up we realized there were so many kinds of soy sauce out there with so much history." - Scott & Tim, Saucyboy's Founders
