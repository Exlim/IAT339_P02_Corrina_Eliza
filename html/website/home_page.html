About SaucyBoys

We're on a mission to make people’s lives saucy! SaucyBoys was an idea that first started when Scott and Tim, a pair of brothers in New York City who wanted to share with their friends how versatile soy sauce can be. Soy sauce has been such an essential for the two growing up. Their mission is to share this amazing stable to every one in the world!

Soy Sauce? What’s that?

Soy sauce has first been made since the 13th century. It has an earthy, umami flavour, making it the ideal all-purpose seasoning! The most basic and key ingredients to Soy Sauce are:

  • Fermented Soybeans
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Wheat
